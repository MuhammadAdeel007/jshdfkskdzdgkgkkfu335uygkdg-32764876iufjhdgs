Frequently Asked Questions

Are the providers on Northbeam employees?

No. Northbeam is a platform that connects consumers with independent service providers. Each provider operates its own business and is responsible for its own licensing, insurance, and work quality.

How are estimate ranges calculated?

Estimate ranges reflect publicly reported national and regional averages for similar projects. Your final cost depends on project scope, materials, location, and the provider you select.

Does Northbeam perform the work?

No. Northbeam does not perform any home services. We help connect you with independent providers who do.

How quickly will I hear back?

Most requests receive responses within one to three business days, depending on your location and project type.
