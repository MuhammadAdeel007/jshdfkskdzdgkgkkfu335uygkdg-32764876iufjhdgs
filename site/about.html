About Northbeam

Connecting America to Independent Service Providers

Our Mission

Make Finding Help Simple, Anywhere

Our mission is straightforward: give every consumer in the United States a single, reliable starting point for finding local service providers — regardless of where they live or what they need.

We do not perform services ourselves. We connect people to the independent professionals who do.

Our Values

What Guides the Platform

Nationwide Access

Coverage across all 50 states, counties, and cities — including rural and underserved markets where finding help can be hardest.

The professionals on our network are independent. They set their own services, schedules, and pricing. Northbeam does not employ them.

Transparency

We never sell rankings, leads, or preferential placement. Consumers decide who to contact based on their own needs.

Accessibility

Platform Overview

How the Platform Is Structured

Northbeam is organized around four layers that work together to support nationwide coverage.

Services

Categorized service types (home, repair, professional, personal, and more). Each category is a doorway into local providers.

States

State-level pages covering every U.S. state. Each page links into counties, cities, and services available there.

Counties and Cities

County and city pages surface the providers and categories most relevant to that specific local market.

Articles and FAQ

Editorial content that helps consumers understand services, plan projects, and make better decisions.

The Matching Process

From Request to Local Provider

Northbeam uses a lightweight, transparent matching process designed to route requests quickly without collecting unnecessary data.

  1. Tell us what you need. Select a service category and share your location (state, county, or city).
  2. Review local options. Browse independent providers serving your area, along with availability and category context.
  3. Connect directly. Reach out to the provider you choose. Communication and agreements happen between you and the provider.
  4. Decide on your terms. Pricing, scope, and scheduling are handled by the provider. Northbeam does not insert itself into the transaction.
